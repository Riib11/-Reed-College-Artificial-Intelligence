Convergence:

if \(\forall 0 \leq k < 1\) we have

$$ \t{dist}\left( \binom{U^A_{i+1}{U^B_{i+1}}} , \binom{U^A}{U^B} \right) \leq k \cdot \t{dist}\left( \binom{U^A_{i}{U^B_{i}}} , \binom{U^A}{U^B} \right), $$

then

$$ \lim_{i \rightarrow \infty} \t{dist}\left( \binom{U^A_{i}{U^B_{i}}} , \binom{U^A}{U^B} \right) = 0 $$

We can get this by showing that

$$ \begin{aligned} \t{dist}\left( \binom{U^A_{i}{U^B_{i}}} , \binom{U^A}{U^B} \right) &= \max_{q \in \{A, B\}} \left| U^q_{i-1} - U^q \right| \\ \max_{q \in \{A, B\}} \left| U^q_{i+1} - U^q \right| &= \left[ R_0(q) + \gamma \max_{\sigma \in \Sigma} \sum_{q\p \in Q} U_i^{q\p}(q\p \mid q, \sigma) \right] - \left[ R_0(q) + \gamma \max_{\sigma \in \Sigma} \sum_{q\p \in Q} U^{q\p}(q\p \mid q, \sigma) \right] \\ &= \left| \gamma \max_{\sigma} \sum_{q\p} U^{q\p}_i P(q\p \mid q, \sigma) - \max_\sigma \sum{q\p} U^{q\p} P(q\p \mid q, \sigma) \right| \\ &\leq \gamma \max_\sigma \left| \sum_{q\p} U^{q\p}_i P(q\p \mid q, \sigma) - \sum_{q\p} U^{q\p} P(q\p \mid q, \sigma) \right| \\ &= \gamma \max_\sigma \left| \sum_{q\p} (U^{q\p}_i - U^{q\p}) P(q\p \mid q, \sigma) \right| \\ &\leq \gamma \max_\sigma \sum_{q\p} |U^{q\p}_i - U^{q\p}| P(q\p \mid q, \sigma) \\ &\leq \gamma \max_\sigma \max_{q\p} | U^{q\p}_i - U^{q\p} | \\ &= \gamma \max_{q\p} | U^{q\p}_i - U^{q\p} | \\ &\leq \t{dist}\left( \binom{U^A_{i}{U^B_{i}}} , \binom{U^A}{U^B} \right) \end{aligned} $$
